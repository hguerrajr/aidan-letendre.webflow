About

Aidan LeTendre is a New York-based filmmaker and recent graduate from New York University's Tisch School of the Arts Film and Television program. Since childhood, Aidan has loved producing, designing, and building things. Whether these words resulted in full-fledged feature-length screenplays, short films, or intricate LEGO models, he has always accepted creation in whatever form it took. In college, Aidan channeled this productive mentality exclusively toward filmmaking. On set, in addition to writing and directing, Aidan is an active cinematographer, gaffer, assistant camera, and grip. He prides himself on being a versatile and adaptable member on set. Working on his own and other students' short films taught him to be both a leader and a team player.In April, Aidan co-wrote and directed his senior thesis film, 'Amnesia.' This project required him to direct and lead a team of over thirty crew members. His meticulous attention to detail, disciplined approach, and extensive pre-production notes were instrumental in leading the team through six consecutive successful shoot days. Aidan's obsessive organizational habits are among his greatest strengths.Aidan loves learning new skills and filling his free time with productivity. Besides his filmmaking ventures, Aidan prioritizes his physical well-being and enjoys strength training, long-distance running, and daily meditation.
